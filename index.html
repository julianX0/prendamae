Para a minha Mãe

Roses are red,
Violets are blue,
Every line I write,
Is for you.
Your smile lights my day,
Your hug keeps me warm,
Through every storm,
You guide my way.
Forever grateful,
Forever true,
Every beat of my heart,
Belongs to you.
